Intro

Greetings, I'm Aakash, a passionate graduate student pursuing a Master's degree in Data Analytics Engineering at George Mason University. With a dynamic background and a zest for data-driven insights, I've had the privilege to gain valuable experience through internships and various projects. Currently, I'm serving as a Policy Analytics Intern at the prestigious Mercatus Center, where I engage in the exciting world of policy analysis, leveraging data to make informed decisions and shape public discourse. During the past summer, I had the pleasure of interning at Nestle USA as a Data Science Intern, where I worked on a diverse range of projects, from forecasting to classification and in-depth data analysis. My contributions extended both as an individual contributor and as a vital team member, harnessing the power of data to drive solutions and deliver results. I'm enthusiastic about the limitless possibilities that data science offers, and I look forward to continually expanding my horizons in this field
